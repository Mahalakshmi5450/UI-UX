Prometheus Fuels

Prometheus Fuels is an American energy startup founded by Rob McGinnis. The Prometheus Fuels' official website, discusses an article on the potential of Direct Air Capture (DAC) technology in addressing climate change. It aims to inform readers about the advancements in DAC and how Prometheus Fuels utilizes this technology to convert atmospheric CO₂ into carbon-neutral fuels. The intention is to raise awareness about innovative solutions to climate challenges and position the company as a leader in sustainable energy.

The website is managed by Prometheus Fuels, a company dedicated to developing technology that removes CO₂ from the air and transforms it into gasoline and jet fuel. Their mission is to create zero net carbon fuels that are cost-competitive with fossil fuels, contributing to a sustainable energy future.

Alexander Venus, a UI/UX/Product Designer, and Roberto Gonzalez, the CTO, worked together to design the Prometheus Fuels website. The website was a nominee for the 2021 Website of the Year award.

The website was published on December 10, 2021. At that time, there was a growing emphasis on climate change mitigation and the development of sustainable technologies. Innovations like DAC were gaining attention as viable solutions to reduce atmospheric CO₂ levels, reflecting a cultural shift towards environmental responsibility and clean energy adoption.

The website employs standard web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to present content effectively. Given its corporate nature, the design focuses on clarity and accessibility, ensuring that information about their technologies and mission is easily digestible for a broad audience.
