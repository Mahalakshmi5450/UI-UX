Joshua's World

Joshua's World is the personal portfolio of Joshua von Hofen, an interaction designer. The website offers an immersive experience by allowing users to explore a 3D island, symbolizing Joshua's journey and projects. As users navigate this virtual environment, they encounter various landmarks representing his educational background and professional work. This interactive design not only showcases his projects but also reflects his enthusiasm for creating engaging digital experiences.

The website was created by Joshua von Hofen, himself; an interaction designer who began his studies in Communication Design in Germany and later pursued a Master's degree in Norway. Currently based in Oslo, Joshua focuses on crafting engaging and delightful digital experiences.

The website was published on the 24th of September, 2022 and went on to get recognized as "Site of the Day" on November 3, 2022, by Awwwards, indicating its creation around that time. During this period, web design trends emphasized immersive user experiences, interactive 3D elements, and personalized storytelling, aligning with the capabilities of modern browsers and the increasing expectations of users for engaging online content.

The website's interactive 3D environment has used web technologies such as WebGL for rendering 3D graphics, along with JavaScript libraries like Three.js. The seamless navigation and animations indicate a focus on responsive design and performance optimization to ensure a smooth user experience across devices. Additionally, Sketchfab users, namely- Matthias Krammer, sycoinc, Argylle, Claqueta, Suushimi, iangchang, and Denys Almaral have provided their 3D assets, like- Low Poly Cyclist, Low Poly Tree Concept, Food Truck, Clapperboard, Low Poly Camper, Robo Bomb, Idle 2 Male for the successful execution of the interactive website.
