Every Last Drop!

"Every Last Drop!" is an interactive website designed to educate users about daily water consumption and the significant impact of small behavioral changes on overall water usage. As users scroll through the site, engaging animations illustrate various aspects of water consumption, highlighting how everyday activities contribute to water waste and offering practical tips for conservation. The website emphasizes that minor adjustments in daily routines can lead to substantial water savings, aiming to raise awareness and encourage responsible water use among individuals.

Nice & Serious, a London-based design firm, created the interactive website "Every Last Drop!" in collaboration with Waterwise.

The site was developed in the mid-2000s and it uses parallax scrolling to tell a story about water waste. The other tools that were used for the development of the site include- HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
